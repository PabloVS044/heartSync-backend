### Create new advertisement
POST http://localhost:3000/api/ads
Content-Type: application/json

{
  "title": "Summer Getaway",
  "description": "Book your dream vacation now!",
  "image": "https://example.com/summer-ad.jpg"
}

### Get advertisement by id
GET http://localhost:3000/api/ads/ad123
Content-Type: application/json

### Archive advertisement
PATCH http://localhost:3000/api/ads/ad123/archive
Content-Type: application/json

### Get archived advertisements
GET http://localhost:3000/api/ads/archived
Content-Type: application/json
